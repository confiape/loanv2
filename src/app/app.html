Features

  •  Multiple sizes: sm, md, lg, xl, 2xl
  •  Dismissible (click outside to close) or non-dismissible
  •  Composable: Header, Body, Footer components
  •  Dark mode support with design tokens
  •  Keyboard navigation (ESC to close when dismissible)
  •  Focus trap for accessibility
  •  Integrates with your Button component
